About us

Welcome to CodeBite, where technology meets taste. We believe that getting great food delivered should be a simple, reliable, and delightful experience. In a world full of options, we’re here to bring you not just any meal, but the right meal—the one that makes your day better.

Our Story

It all started in Milan in late 2022 over a meal that was memorable for all the wrong reasons: a cold pizza and a frustrating delivery app. Our founders, Elena, a third-generation Milanese restaurateur, and Marco, a software engineer with a passion for efficiency, knew there had to be a better way. Elena knew the city's hidden culinary gems—the family-run trattorias and innovative kitchens that locals loved but were often invisible on mainstream platforms. Marco knew he could build the technology to bridge that gap. He envisioned a smart, streamlined system that would connect hungry customers with amazing local chefs, ensuring every meal arrived hot, fresh, and on time. They combined their expertise to create CodeBite: a platform built on sophisticated Code to deliver the perfect Bite. We launched in Milan in the summer of 2023, expanded to Rome and Turin in 2024, and since then, we've been on a mission to change the way people experience their city's food.
